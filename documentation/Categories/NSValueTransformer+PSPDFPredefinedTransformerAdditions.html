Overview

Ensure an NSNumber is backed by __NSCFBoolean/CFBooleanRef

NSJSONSerialization, and likely other serialization libraries, ordinarily serialize NSNumbers as numbers, and thus booleans would be serialized as 0/1. The exception is when the NSNumber is backed by __NSCFBoolean, which, though very much an implementation detail, is detected and serialized as a proper boolean.
