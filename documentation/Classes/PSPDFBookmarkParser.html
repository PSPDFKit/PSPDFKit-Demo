Inherits from NSObject
Declared in PSPDFBookmarkParser.h

Overview

Manages bookmarks for a PSPDFDocument.

There is no notion of “bookmarks” in a PDF. (PDF “bookmarks” are entries in the Table Of Contents; which are parsed in PSPDFKit by the PSPDFOutlineParser class)

Bookmarks are saved in /Library/PrivateDocuments//bookmark.plist

All calls are thread safe.
