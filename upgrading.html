Upgrade from PSPDFKit v1

If you already own a license for PSPDFKit v1, you can upgrade your license to PSPDFKit v2 with paying just 50% of the regular price.
You need to accept the new PSPDFKit v2 license with your purchase. (one license = one app bundle ID)


If you purchased your license before March 30, 2012, please contact me directly for upgrading.


If your license includes a free update to PSPDFKit v2, you will receive an email with the updated links shortly. If you can't wait, you can also contact me directly to get access.


In any case, upgrading is not yet automated, so contact me for your upgrade code at peter@pspdfkit.com
