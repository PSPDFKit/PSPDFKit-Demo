Why choose PSPDFKit?

Heavily Optimized

PSPDFKit is FAST. With its innovative caching, your documents will be displayed almost instantly. And thanks to the new improvements in v2 even zoomed content will be rendered super-fast. Gone are the days where you could see single blocks rendered at a time, like Safari does.

Customizable

This is not your typical Apple class. Almost every aspect of PSPDFKit is customizable. A lot of thought went into the design of the API. PSPDFKit also exposes a lot of hooks that make subclassing easier.

Easy Integration

Integrating our iOS PDF library into your own app is super easy and takes about 5 minutes. Focus on the unique parts of your product and build on a well-documented codebase that is heavily field-tested and just works.

Features

  • Multiple transition styles: magazine-page scrolling, iBooks-like pageCurl, Safari-like scrolling.

  • Text selection and search highlighting.

  • Annotations read and write support (Highlight, Notes, Ink drawing, ...).

  • A tabbed controller to show multiple pdf's and persists state.

  • Multimedia annotations support (using link annotations and the pspdfkit:// url scheme. Video, audio, image and web browser support).

  • PDF Outline/Table Of Contents parsing & display.

  • Single or double page view support, including automatic mode that changes on landscape switch.

  • Optional setting to show first page in single mode, everything else in two pages. (magazine style)

  • Support for both horizontal (magazine style) and vertical (UIWebView) scrolling mode.

  • Pinch to Zoom / Double Tap to to zoom in and out.

  • Tap right for next page, Tap left for previous page.

  • Fast Thumbnail extraction, grid display and caching.

  • Intelligent caching features - you control how much should be cached.

  • Automatic page sizing via PDF crop-box metadata. Multiple aspect-ratios per document supported.

  • Delegates to fine-control pdf display, with callbacks for custom overlays.

  • Logical PDF container (PSPDFDocument), lets you combine multiple PDFs into one big pdf.

  • Fully customizable interface: You can disable features like search, thumbnails or the whole HUD and replace it with your own.

  • Highly Multi-Threaded. Grand Central Dispatch is used throughout the framework to ensure maximum speed, and use every core available.

  • Optimized for both iPad and iPhone, fully compatible with iOS5 and iOS6.

  • Read the CHANGELOG for a full list of features and changes.
