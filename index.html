Why choose PSPDFKit?

Time to Market

PSPDFKit supports fast PDF viewing, annotating and signing of documents. It's battle-tested code is being used in hundredths of apps. You can benefit from a solid codebase and incorporate all those advanced features into your own applications. It will save you at least one year of production time in comparison with in-house development.

Customizable

Almost every aspect of PSPDFKit is customizable and designed from the ground up for maximum flexibility. The API exposes a lot of features that will cover just about every use case you can think of. And special subclassing hooks make it super simple to customize the views with minimal effort.

Easy Integration

Integrating our iOS PDF library into your application takes about 5 minutes. Focus on the unique parts of your product and build on a well-documented codebase that is heavily field-tested and just works.
